Pizza Time!

Help the chef make a pizza by collecting the toppings that you see on the menu - in order!

Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move the chef left and right, catching the toppings (i.e. letting them hit you) listed on the menu, and dodging those that aren't.

Once you've collected them correctly, click the button that appears to take you to the next level. If you've collected incorrect toppings, try again!

Collect the right ingredients for all three levels/pizzas, and you win!
